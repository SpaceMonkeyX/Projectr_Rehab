Introduction

Ankur Rehab Center have come up with this novel idea of setting up a rehab center for catering psychiatric and/or addiction problem by highly expertise profession personals exclusively based on the specific need of the family members & their ward so that they can experience the delightful stay with quality care, comfort, security, kindness, love & empathy as provided by their family along with rehabilitation. The easily approachable outskirt area, away from city pollution & congestion, spreads in about One Lack sq. ft. out of which only 7000*3 sq.ft constructed area is utilized for stay, rest adjacent area is surrounded by lush green, agricultural & horticultural farms & lands becomes one of the unique feature of the center which provides a very healthy, clean, green, calm n quite, stress free environment to the residents.

Current Scenario

In a family psychiatric and/or addiction problem comes as a curse and for those who have less understanding and lack of manpower, this problem becomes a fatal problem. In a nut-shell each and every family having mentally ill member needs extra support, care, treatment, medicine, guidance; rehabilitation & re-organization for their ward & to some extent for themselves also.

Requirement of Rehabilitation

The situation becomes worst where there is no care taker is available, lack of facilities, family members or siblings are not interested in taking care for long duration, nuclear families and some other kind of such situations. Even after giving best available medicines why results are not measurable? Does family give them medicines in time? Does family give them support and care of the required extent? Does family give quality times to them? These are some questions which can be solved by providing required expertise professional special care and rehabilitation assistance to the mentally ill & addicted persons and their family member.
